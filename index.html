Lemon’s Art Gallery

Healing • Expression • Light

I am an artist who believes that art is not decoration — it is refuge.

Art helped me come out of depression. In moments when words failed, colors stayed. Brushes listened. Paint held me together.

This space exists so that no one feels alone in their darkness. You don’t have to be perfect to create. You only need to feel.

Touch the paint. Feel the colors. Pour your heart onto walls, canvas, floors — anything. Let art become your light.

Wall & Space Art

Art for Everyone

You don’t need talent, training, or permission. Art belongs to anyone who feels deeply.

Why Lemon?

Like lemons, life can be sharp, overwhelming, and intense. But even the sharpest things carry healing, freshness, and light.

Lemon’s Art Gallery is a reminder that brightness can exist alongside pain — and sometimes because of it.
